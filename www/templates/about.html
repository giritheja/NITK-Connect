NITK Connect

NITK Connect is a platform where all students of NITK can get access to information related to NITK college life. Every student club or community is represented on the app and can share news related to current activities. It aims to bridge the gap between all NITKians, and allows us to share ideas in our college. NITK Connect is an app for the students, which is why we have convinced the best restaurants nearby to give special discount offers for NITK students. NITK Connect will continue to improve with more features and become more engaging with every update.
